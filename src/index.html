About Our Hotel

Welcome to Dusvi Star Hotel, where luxury meets comfort. Located in the heart of Galle, our hotel offers a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers seeking relaxation and indulgence.

At Dusvi Star Hotel, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and hospitality. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring your stay is nothing short of perfect.

Our hotel features luxurious accommodations, ranging from elegant guest rooms to spacious suites, each meticulously designed to provide the utmost comfort and convenience. From modern amenities to stunning views of the city skyline, every detail is carefully curated to exceed your expectations.

Indulge your senses with our exquisite dining options, offering a diverse selection of culinary delights prepared by our award-winning chefs. Whether you're craving international cuisine or local specialties, our restaurants and bars provide a culinary journey like no other.

Unwind and rejuvenate at our state-of-the-art spa and wellness center, where you can pamper yourself with a range of treatments and therapies designed to relax the mind, body, and soul. Or, take a dip in our sparkling pool and soak up the sun in our lush tropical gardens.

Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate event, Dusvi Star Hotel offers an unparalleled experience that will create lasting memories. Come discover the essence of luxury and hospitality at Dusvi Star Hotel.
